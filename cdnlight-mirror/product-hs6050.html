Product description

The series is developed with family-oriented planning and cut-out size selections including 40mm, 50mm, 70mm, 90mm and 125mm.

The Fermat optical system suppresses stray light, supports a clear and rounded light spot and smooth colour transition.

The 360° eagle-eye drive mechanism allows one light body to work as both fixed downlight and adjustable downlight.

Optical accessories are available to adjust light spot shape, UGR and other technical parameters.
